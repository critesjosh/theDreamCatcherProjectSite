The Dream Catcher Project

The Dream Catcher Project is the result of an idea to develop a tool to enhance creativity using altered states of consciousness. The idea is not original, but was inspired by techniques used by famous creatives like Thomas Edison and Salvidor Dali.

This project underscores my interest in adopting technologies for unique purposes and creating unusual user experiences.

I began the project by building a simple handheld standalone prototype that worked extremely well, but I wanted to make something that could be easily adopted by anyone who wanted to try it. My early protypes quickly developed into a smartphone application.

I developed a functional version of the application and it worked suprisingly well. To create a commercial prototype to publish in the app store will take quite a bit more work and I attempted to raise money with a Kickstarter campaign. Unfortunately I did not meet my fundraising goal and learned how important marketing and outreach are for the success of a product.

The project is currently on hold as I develop my programming skills and pursue other projects, but I hope to come back to this project in the future and build the awesome product that I know it can be.
